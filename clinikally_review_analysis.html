Root cause — shared across all three

Post-purchase operations are broken. No functioning support line. No proactive delivery communication. No exception-handling for fulfillment failures. Discovery and checkout clearly work — the failure starts the moment an order leaves the cart.

Instagram signals — post-purchase failure confirmed across channels

Clinikally's Instagram comments surface three signals that the Play Store data couldn't — a named delivery partner, a supply chain integrity failure, and evidence that support is broken across every channel simultaneously.

SUPPLY CHAIN

A commenter names Elastic Run explicitly as the delivery partner — reporting rude behaviour, refusal to hand over the package without cash payment upfront, and aggressive conduct at the customer's door. CRITICAL

If confirmed, it represents a supply chain integrity gap on a clinical product that directly impacts patient safety.

A loyal customer (4 orders) lost permanently. If this is a warehouse-level substitution pattern, it is a safety and regulatory exposure, not a one-off logistics miss.
