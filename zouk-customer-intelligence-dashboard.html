The product isn't the problem — quality and craftsmanship are the most-praised, most-upvoted themes in the dataset. What's breaking is everything after checkout: delivery reliability, and even more consistently, how support responds once delivery fails.

Why the timing matters

Zouk went live on Myntra's M-Now quick-commerce program in April 2026, leaning into gifting and last-minute, occasion-led purchases as a growth story — the same month this dataset's negative sentiment begins its climb, driven substantially by delayed gifts and expedited-delivery promises that reviewers say weren't honored on Zouk's own channels.

Zouk is also currently raising a pre-Series C round, with FY26 financials not yet public. Neither fact is necessarily connected to the other — but both raise the stakes on fixing fulfillment and support before the speed-and-gifting promise gets pushed harder.
